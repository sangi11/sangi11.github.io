Greatest weightlifters by Sinclair score

1. Naim Suleymanoglu

Also known as the 'Pocket Hercules' due to his small stature but incredible strength. At just 4' 10" in height, and 60kg in bodyweight, Naim lifted 152.5kg in the snatch and 190kg in the clean and jerk, giving him a total of 342.5kg and a Sinclair score of 500.7047

2. Lasha Talakhadze

Lasha Talakhadze is the only weightlifter to come close to Suleymanoglu, and may surpass him in the future. Weighing 182kg Talakhadze competes in the superheavyweight division and holds the all time world records of 225kg in the snatch, 267kg in the clean and jerk and 492kg in the total giving him a Sinclair score of 492.27
